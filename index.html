About Us

LINK (Local Integrated Network of Kerala IEEE Students) is an innovative concept that originated in IEEE Kerala Section in India, aimed at networking student branches in an effective way, thereby increasing the value in IEEE Student membership. LINK was formally inaugurated by past IEEE President Mr. Cleon Anderson in January 2005. Since its inception LINK has achieved tremendous results, causing an exponential increase in student branches and student activities in IEEE Kerala Section over the years. The model of link was adopted by IEEE Region 10 as R10 GINI (Global Integrated Network of IEEE Students).

Why Join IEEE LINK Camp

Join the premier student leadership event that empowers you to lead, innovate, and grow. IEEE LINK Camp is your gateway to understanding the vast opportunities within IEEE and shaping the future of student activities in Kerala.

  • Leadership & Management: Master the art of leading teams and managing large-scale initiatives.
  • Technical Exposure: Get hands-on with the latest tools and technologies driving the industry.
  • Networking: Connect with like-minded peers, industry professionals, and IEEE leaders.
  • Personal Growth: Enhance your soft skills, confidence, and professional etiquette.
  • Impact: Make a meaningful contribution to the vibrant IEEE Kerala Section student community.
